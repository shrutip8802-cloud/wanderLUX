About WanderLux

Our story, mission, and the people behind your trip

Who We Are

WanderLux Travel Agency was founded in 2024 by a small group of seasoned travellers who shared a single belief: travel should be easy, exciting, and personal. From a single office in Sydney we now serve customers across Australia, helping them plan everything from weekend escapes to round-the-world adventures.

We are a fully accredited travel agency and members of the Australian Federation of Travel Agents (AFTA). All our consultants have personally visited the destinations they recommend, so the advice you receive is based on real experience – not just brochures.

Our Mission

To turn travel dreams into stress-free reality. We do this by listening carefully to what each customer wants, designing itineraries that fit their life and budget, and supporting them at every step of the journey.

Our Values

Honesty: We give straightforward advice – including telling you when a cheaper option is better.

Care: Every customer is treated as an individual, not a booking number.

Reliability: If something goes wrong while you are away, we are reachable 24/7.

Sustainability: We promote operators that respect local communities and the environment.
